Assessment 09: Emotional Life Analysis

The Premise

"Our study of modern relationships suggests that moving toward non-monogamy is not necessarily 'liberation,' but rather a sophisticated strategy to avoid the heavy lifting of deep emotional intimacy. By spreading your needs across many different people, you effectively avoid the difficult, one-on-one work required to face your own internal insecurities. We view this as a way to avoid being overwhelmed by the weight of your own feelings."

The Question

"As someone living this way, we invite you to critique this idea. Is our assessment a cynical misinterpretation of your freedom, or have you noticed, among your own circles, that keeping things complex is actually a way to avoid truly knowing yourself?"
